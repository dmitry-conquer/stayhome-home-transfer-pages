Seamless Transfer of HomeCare Services to Stay at Home Homecare

It is within the patient’s rights to select any Homecare agency of their choice. If you or your caregiver are dissatisfied with the quality of services provided by your current Homecare agency, the patient has the right to choose any agency for any reason.

Please note that transferring homecare services to another agency can be a difficult process if not done properly. Inexperienced agencies may mishandle the transfer, leading to gaps in patient services, and caregivers may not get paid for several days or even longer. At Stay at Home Homecare, we understand the importance of a proper transfer. Our experienced coordinators will ensure that your caregiver does not lose any work hours during the transfer and the patient will not miss any of the services provided to them. We strive to make the transfer process as seamless and painless as possible for you and your loved ones.
